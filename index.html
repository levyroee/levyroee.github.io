I am a senior lecturer (assistant professor) at the Tel Aviv University School of Economics. I also serve as a Research Fellow at CEPR Political Economy. Before joining Tel Aviv University, I visited MIT as a post-doc and received my PhD from Yale University.

My research focuses on political economy and social media. I am especially interested in studying the forces shaping social norms, political attitudes, and policy preferences, and their subsequent influence on individual behavior.
